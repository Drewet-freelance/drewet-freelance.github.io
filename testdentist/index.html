Laurelhurst Dentistry

Dentist In Portland OR

Welcome to Laurelhurst Dentistry, where we have been providing quality dental care to the Portland community for over 20 years. We believe getting to know our patients is at the heart of providing the best dental experience possible. We have created a warm, welcoming environment where your comfort and dental health are our top priorities. At Laurelhurst Dentistry, we focus on prevention and providing the highest quality, state-of-the-art dental care. We believe communication is key, and we will listen and work with you to develop a practical and successful dental plan to meet your needs.
